At Microsoft, our mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. Our values align to our mission, support our culture, and serve as a declaration of how we treat each other, our customers, and our partners. They include respect, integrity and accountability. Respect means we recognize that the thoughts, feelings, and backgrounds of others are as important as our own. Integrity means we are honest, ethical, and trustworthy. Accountability means we accept full responsibility for our decisions, actions, and results.
